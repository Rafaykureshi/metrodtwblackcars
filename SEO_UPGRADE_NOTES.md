# Metro DTW SEO Upgrade

This version preserves the updated booking, Supabase, Resend, fleet, and design changes while adding:

- Enhanced Next.js metadata, canonical URLs, Open Graph, Twitter cards
- LocalBusiness/TaxiService, WebSite, FAQPage, Service, and Breadcrumb JSON-LD
- Dynamic sitemap and robots rules
- FAQ and linked service-area sections on the homepage
- PWA manifest, icons, favicon, and social sharing image
- `noindex` protection for admin pages
- Page-specific metadata for book, services, privacy, and terms
- Compression, modern image formats, HSTS, and removal of the X-Powered-By header
- Sanitized `.env.example` with no real secrets

No SEO work can guarantee a #1 Google ranking. Ranking also depends on Google Business Profile, reviews, backlinks, content quality, competition, and time.
